About me

Kanchan is a dedicated data scientist who believes in using data to drive more informed and impactful decisions. Guided by her commitment to open data and open-source software, she strives to make code more accessible and enable efficient data utilization across industries. While her primary focus is on Python, she also brings experience in R and Java to her work.

Through her work experience at leading companies such as Boeing India Pvt. Ltd. and Givetastic, Germany; Kanchan has honed her skills in computer vision, deep learning, MLOps and compliance. Kanchan earned her bachelor's degree from Cummins College of Engineering for Women in Pune, where she delved into data science, computer science, and mathematical optimization, building a strong foundation for her career.
