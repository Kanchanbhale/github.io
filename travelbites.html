Framework for production cycle time optimization and correction.

CONCEPT

My work as Boeing Machine Learing Intern involved developing an end to end system for cycle time prediction for optimizing the production cycle pipeline. Reducing the man-hours not only promises an efficient move towards resource planning but also provides highly reliable and accurate autonomous solution.

PROTOTYPE

I used generative pre-trained transformers for production architecture optimization by cycle time prediction during wing fabrication, my approach involved utilizing the temporal nature of procedural data in 3p5m production datasheets. Driven by the pursuit of an innovative solution, I conducted numerous experiments for several days by modifying the transformerâ€™s attention network. By meticulously fine-tuning the model on an intermediate cross-keyword semantic framework, I was not only able to achieve an incredible accuracy of 96.2%, much higher than the baseline accuracy of 58.27%, but also proposed notable co-relation between factory work quality and cycle time to support revenue savings. My work resulted in the first-ever complex data science framework, which was not only presented to technical leaders but was also granted a distinction of a US IP.
